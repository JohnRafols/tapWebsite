Contact

 

A year ago an online store owner came to the coworking space that was ran by Mariya, our Founder. The space was known for it's creative community. He asked if we know any designers who could help with his website. Soon we learned that he already spent days looking for a designer with the right style and skills, willing to work to his budget and time-frames.

For him, this is days of productivity and business loss. Knowing that there are plenty of qualified designers interested in freelance work and small businesses looking for them, we wanted to find our why this was stressful and took him so long.

We decided to talk to startups and SMEs from creative, tech and media sectors. We found out they would spend hours online and asking their friends before finding the right creative for their one-off and short-term projects. We also realized that most of these companies didn't have clear project tracking channels and questioned the quality of the finals that they received. There isn't a centralized hub for companies to connect to a large pool of quality creators that is fast, on-demand and can work with any budget. As the result, we created Tap.
